@njspotlightnews October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month. Earlier this year, Alanna Vizzoni, a 27-year-old Hoboken resident, was stunned when she was diagnosed with Stage II invasive ductal carcinoma breast cancer. Vizzoni isn’t alone in her experience. A new report from the American Cancer Society finds rates of breast cancer are rising fastest in women under the age of 50 and instances increased overall 1% each year from 2012–2021. Vizzoni has turned to her social media followers to share her cancer journey and urge other young women to get screened.
